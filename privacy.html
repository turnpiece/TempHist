Privacy Policy

Effective date: September 2025

TempHist, operated by Turnpiece Ltd., respects your privacy.

No personal data collected

TempHist does not collect, store, or share any personal information.

Location use

If you grant permission, the app uses your current location once to retrieve historical weather data for your area. Location data is never shared but is temporarily stored in a cookie on your machine for one hour.

Third-party services

TempHist uses Firebase for anonymous authentication and may set third-party cookies from Google services. These cookies are used solely for authentication purposes and do not track personal information.

No tracking or analytics

The app does not include analytics, advertising or third-party tracking beyond the authentication service mentioned above.

Data sources

Weather and climate data are provided via the TempHist API, which sources historical weather data from trusted providers. Requests are processed anonymously.

Contact

If you have questions, please contact Turnpiece Ltd. at https://turnpiece.com.
